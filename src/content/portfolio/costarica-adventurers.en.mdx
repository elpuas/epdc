        ## The Challenge

        The project started from scratch. *There was no website*, content structure, or a *defined technology base*. The challenge was to build a platform that could:

        - Communicate the value proposition clearly
        - Operate in multiple languages
        - Be easy to manage without technical knowledge
        - Grow alongside the business

        ## Our Solution

        We built a *completely new WordPress site*, using a custom theme tailored to the *needs of the tourism project*. We implemented a clear *multilingual architecture*, simple navigation, and a structure focused on building trust and making it easy for potential clients to get in touch.

        Beyond the technical build, *we supported the client throughout the entire process*, helping define content, structure, and best practices for everyday site use.
