Below you'll find answers to the most common questions clients and agencies ask us about WordPress development, WooCommerce, web support, white-label services, and technical SEO.

### 1. What's included in a custom WordPress website?
We *build custom websites tailored to your needs*. This includes responsive design, SEO best practices, fast load times, and a user-friendly admin interface using block editor or ACF as needed. Hosting, security, and ongoing support can be included.

### 2. Do you develop online stores with WooCommerce?
Yes. We specialize in *building WooCommerce* stores that are conversion-focused, optimized for speed, and include custom features like inventory sync, one-page checkout, and third-party API integrations.

### 3. What kind of support and maintenance do you offer?
We offer *monthly maintenance plans* that cover security updates, plugin and core updates, uptime monitoring, backups, performance optimization, and dev hours for small tasks. Support is direct and fast via Slack or email.

### 5. Can you help with SEO?
Yes. We *implement technical SEO foundations* like proper heading structure, performance tuning, semantic HTML, metadata, Open Graph tags, and structured data. We also help with keyword mapping and site architecture.

### 6. How long does a website project usually take?
Depending on scope, a basic site can be done in 2–4 weeks. More complex e-commerce or multilingual projects take 6–8 weeks or more. *We provide detailed timelines in our proposals.*

### 7. Can you redesign my existing website?
Yes. We often take *existing websites and rebuild them* using modern tools like Astro, WordPress block themes, or hybrid stacks. We improve performance, UX, and maintain your content.

### 8. What makes your service different?
We *combine speed, performance, and communication*. Every project is *handcrafted with care*, and we use *AI-assisted workflows* to streamline delivery without cutting corners. You get direct access to expert developers.

### 9. Where are you based?
Our team is based in *Costa Rica, USA, and Italy*. We serve clients worldwide and work across time zones. *Communication is always fast and clear.*
